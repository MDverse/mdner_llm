KKEEVKKEEV(PK)7KEEVKKEEVKK (charged) disordered linker simulations in 10mM CaCl2 with AMBER99SB-DISP

Classical MD simulations of the disordered linker with sequence KKEEVKKEEV(PK)7KEEVKKEEVKK (charged) disordered linker using AMBER99SB-DISP force field, run in 10mM CaCl2.Five replicas (mentioned as model01,02,03,04,05) were run with five different initial structures generated with IDPconfGen (Teixeira et al. (2022)).
