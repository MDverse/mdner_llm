MD simulation trajectory of a lipid bilayer: 70/30 mol% POPC/Cholesterol . SLIPIDS, Gromacs 4.6.3. 2016.
MD simulation trajectory files, for fully hydrated POPC + CHOLESTEROL bilayer (70/30 mol%) [358 POPC, 154 CHOL, 21183 WAT]. The SLIPIDS force field was used with Gromacs 4.6.3. Conditions: T=298K. 170 ns each trajectory, last 100 ns analyzed.
