ACS Omega - In silico potential of bergenin complexed in β-cyclodextrin and AI-designed derivates in diabetic neuropathy pathway

 




Here you will find the raw data from the research involving the molecular dynamics of protein-ligand complex with receptors involved in diabetic neuropathy, which includes: 
 
P2Y12 + Selatogrel complex (PDB ID: 7PPI) in a 200 simulation ns on Desmond by Schrodinger Maestro 2021-2 (Linux version) 
 
P2Y12 + ACD5b complex (PDB ID: 7PPI), corresponding to the nitrobenzyl derivative of bergenin, in a 200 ns simulation on Desmond by Schrodinger Maestro 2021-2 (Linux version) 
 
P2Y12 + Bergenin complex (PDB ID: 4NTJ), which corresponds to the bergenin molecule interacting with P2Y12, in a 200 ns simulation on Desmond by Schrodinger Maestro 2021-2 (Linux version) 
 
P2X3 + Bergenin complex (PDB ID: 5SVR), which corresponds to the bergenin molecule interacting with P2X3, in a 200 ns simulation on Desmond by Schrodinger Maestro 2021-2 (Linux version) 
 
P2X3 + ACD5b complex (PDB ID: 5SVR), which corresponds to the bergenin molecule interacting with P2X3, in a 200 ns simulation on Desmond by Schrodinger Maestro 2021-2 (Linux version) 
 
Note: These results can be viewed in the academic version of Desmond, which includes the Schrodinger Maestro graphical interface, as well as in the open-source software VMD (Visual Molecular Dynamics).
