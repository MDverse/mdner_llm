Raw data for: Crosstalk between regulatory elements in the disordered TRPV4 N-terminus modulates lipid-dependent channel activity
This repository contains raw data and code related to "Crosstalk between regulatory elements in the disordered TRPV4 N-terminus modulates lipid-dependent channel activity" by Goretzki et al.
Included are molecular dynamics parameter files, initial structures after system equilibration, production trajectories, and final structures of simulations of:
1. coarse-grained IDRs on membrane, and force and position data for restrained c-termini. Trajectories are subsampled with one frame every 50 ns.
2. The tetrameric TRPV4 core in a POPC in atomistic resolution. Trajectories are sampled with one frame every 1 ns. 
3. The isolated ARD in solution in atomistic resolution. Trajectories are subsampled with one frame every 1 ns.
