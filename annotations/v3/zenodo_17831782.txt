disaccharides_2D_PES/Contains scripts to perform dimer potential energy scans (PES).


32 scripts cover different scan directions; for each grid point, only the lowest‑energy conformation is retained.


Scripts in this directory generate the PES for the ADMPC dimer in implicit water.


Example commands:
charmm -i malt_xy_rama.inp -o malt_xy_rama.out
cmap_allinone.awk */*.cmap > am2cycl24.cmap
