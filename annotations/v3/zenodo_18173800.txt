Dataset for "FANAzyme catalysed RNA cleavage is based on a two-state transition"
Data set for publication on simulation of small FANAzyme:RNA cleavage
01_Setup: setup with LEAP and AMBER ff library files
02_MDtrajectories: MD trajectories for complex
03_ELdatabase: Files composing the energy landscape database created with discrete pathsampling
04_Analysis: Analysis scripts used for the computational data analysis
05_SAXS: SAXS data and comparison with simulated SAXS curves
