GROMACS simulations of unfolding of ubiqutin in a strong electric field
The simulations were performed in GROMACS 4.6.7. The dataset contains binary run input files (.tpr) and simulated trajectory files (.trr). Here are 100 statistically independent runs for the field values: E = 3e4 V/nm, E = 5e4 V/nm, E = 7e4 V/nm, E = 11e4 V/nm. The names of files denote the value of the electric field in [V/nm] and the number of independent runs.
