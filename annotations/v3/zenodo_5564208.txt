Dataset (VIII) related to publication: Decisive Role of Water and Protein Dynamics in Residence Time of p38a MAP Kinase Inhibitors
MD simulation data of compound 1 in MSM 2-S3 conformations of the related to the publication Pantsar et al.: Decisive Role of Water and Protein Dynamics in Residence Time of p38a MAP Kinase Inhibitors.
Individual .zip files contain raw-desmond trajectories (-out.cms files and trj-files).
