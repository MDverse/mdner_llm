MD Simulation Data Upload – LC8/nNOS
Naming convention: {PDB ID}{bound state abbr.}{peptide length}_{LC8 species}{number of residues per monomer}_{MD simulation time}_md{replicate}_dt{time step between frames in ps} For example, "1CMIs8_dros89_1us_md1_dt200" is a single replicate (out of three) 178-residue LC8 homodimer singly bound to an 8-residue nNOS peptide for a 1µs trajectory strided at 200ps. 
The 8-residue doubly bound complex had two replicates that were not run to completion and an additional two replicates (...md4 and ...md5) were added in their place.
