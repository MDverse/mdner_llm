Computational studies on the structure of peroxidasin (PXDN) and its interactions
This repository contains computational analyses on peroxidasin (PXDN).

Structural model of homo-trimeric PXDN complex (generated applying AlphaFold2)
Molecular dynamics simulations on putative protein-protein interactions of PXDN

All data in this repository was generated in relation to the folowing publication.

Sung, Hsu-Min et al. Peroxidasin controls melanoma aggressiveness by modulating NK cell-mediated cytotoxicity. Manuscript under revision. 2024
