100ns REST2 MD Simulation Trajectory of Wild-Type Alpha-Synuclein using the DES-Amber Forcefield
Molecular dynamics simulation trajectory and  for wild-type acylated alpha-Synuclein (Ac-AS) using the REST2 algorithm. The system was parameterised with the DES-Amber force field published by Maxwell et al. The simulation was run for 100 nanoseconds using GROMACS on the Forschungszentrum Jülich JUWELS supercomputer. The reposited trajectory is the lowest temperature replica at 300 K from 32 replicas between 300-500 K.
