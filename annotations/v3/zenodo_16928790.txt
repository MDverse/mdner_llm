KKEEVKKEEV(PK)7KEEVKKEEVKK (charged) disordered linker simulations with AMBER99SB_DISP
Classical MD simulations of the disordered linker with sequence KKEEVKKEEV(PK)7KEEVKKEEVKK (charged) disordered linker using AMBER99SB_DISP force field, run in water. Five replicas (mentioned as model01,02,03,04,05) were run with five different initial structures generated with IDPconfGen (Teixeira et al. (2022)).
