Research data supporting: ''Reciprocity in dynamics of supramolecular biosystems for the clustering of ligands and receptors''

This repository contains the set of data and the code to reproduce the Molecular Dynamics (MD) results shown in: ''Reciprocity in dynamics of supramolecular biosystems  for the clustering of ligands and receptors'' published on PNAS (DOI: 10.1073/pnas.2500686122)
