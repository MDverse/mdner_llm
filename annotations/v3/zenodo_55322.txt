Simulation files for DPPC lipid membrane with Slipids force field for Gromacs MD simulation engine
the files denoted with 350 resp 700 resp 1000 have NaCl concentrations of 850 resp 1750 resp 2570 mM.
 
The goal was to study the effect of salt on the order parameters of the lipid head group and the glycerol backbone for the NMRlipids project, see http://nmrlipids.blogspot.fi for more information.
