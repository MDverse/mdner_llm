TonB C-terminal domain simulation with amberff03ws (5 replicas)
MD simulations of the folded C-terminal domain of TonB (residues 195-285) with amberff03ws force field. Five replicas from different starting structures from taken from RCSB PDB: 5LW8 (model 1, 5, 10, 15 and 20). Simulations were run wtih 40 mM NaCl at 303 K. The trajectory has a saving frequency of 10 ps.
