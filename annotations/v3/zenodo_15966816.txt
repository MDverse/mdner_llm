Simulations of Aciniform (wrapping) Silk Repetitive Domain (AcSp1)
Simulations of AcSp1 using the primary sequence of a single unit of the repetitive domain from the following publication aiming to compare simulated and experimental spin relaxation times:
Tremblay, M., Xu, L., Sarker, M., Liu, X., & Rainey, J. (2016). Characterizing Aciniform silk repetitive domain backbone dynamics and hydrodynamic modularity. International Journal of Molecular Sciences, 17(8), 1305. https://doi.org/10.3390/ijms17081305
Simulations were run using 5 models generated with IDPconfGen (Teixeira et al. (2022)) and 5 force fields:
