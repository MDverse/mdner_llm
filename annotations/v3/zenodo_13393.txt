POPC @ 298K, Model by Kukol
Files required for a simulation of a POPC bilayer and the resulting trajectory. The POPC model is that by Andreas Kukol [1]. The structure and topology files are downloaded from Lipidbook [2].
This data is used in the project "Matching lipid force fields with NMR data", see: http://nmrlipids.blogspot.fi 
[1] A Kukol, Lipid Models for United-Atom Molecular Dynamics Simulations of Proteins,  J Chem Theory Comput 5 (2009), 615–626, doi: 10.1021/ct8003468
[2] http://lipidbook.bioch.ox.ac.uk/package/show/id/15.html
