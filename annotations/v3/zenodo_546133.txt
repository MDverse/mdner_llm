MD simulation trajectory of a fully hydrated POPG bilayer: SLIPIDS, Gromacs 5.0.4. 2017.
MD simulation trajectory files, for fully hydrated POPG bilayer [288 POPG, 10664 WAT and 288 NA ions]. The SLIPIDS force field was used with Gromacs 5.0.4. Conditions: T=298K. 250 ns trajectory, last 100 ns uploaded for analysis.
