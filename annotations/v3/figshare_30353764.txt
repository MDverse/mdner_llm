Data for Shuanghuanglian Oral Liquid in SALI Mouse Model”
This dataset contains the Molecular docking and molecular dynamics (MD) simulations supporting the findings of our study titled 'Shuanghuanglian Oral Liquid alleviates sepsis-induced acute lung injury: A study of potential mechanisms involving the CXCL8/NF-κB axis based on network pharmacology and experimental validation'. The data corresponds to the experiments described in the manuscript.
