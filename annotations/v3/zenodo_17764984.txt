Dataset for: Glycosylation-Modulated Conformational Diversity in Neurotrophin Receptors

This repository contains raw data and code related to "Glycosylation-Modulated Conformational Diversity in Neurotrophin Receptors" by Tsengenes, A., Athanasiou, C., and Wade, R., 2025.
Included are molecular dynamics parameter files, initial structures, scripts and structures shown in the figures of the manuscript.
