Simulations of bovine rhodopsin

This repository contains the simulations of phosphorylated and unphosphorylated bovine rhodopsin (Uniprot ID: P02699). The simulations and their analysis were published in 'Proteins: Structure, Function, and Bioinformatics'.
For any use please cite:

Damodaran, K. et al.New simulation insights on the structural transition mechanism of bovine rhodopsin activation.Proteins 91, 771–780 (2023).
