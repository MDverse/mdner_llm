MD simulation trajectory for Heterogenous lipid bilayer with 150mM NaCl concentration

Equilibrated symmetric heterogenous lipid bilayer simulation ran with Gromacs 2020.4, Force field= Charmm36m, 300ns, T=300K, composed of 152 POPC, 96 POPE, 20 POPS, 80 CHOL, 36 PSM, and 16 GM1 molecules, 92 Na+, 56 Cl- , 19348 water (TIP3P) molecules.
