CG simulations of eVP40-Membrane systems
This list contains the .xtc (compressed trajectory) and .gro (coordinate) files for five different coarse-grained systems: 
1) eVP40 & membrane (without PA) 
2) eVP40 & membrane (with 2% PA)
3) eVP40 & membrane (with 7% PA)
4) membrane only (without PA)
5) membrane only (with 7% PA)
The trajectories uploaded here are saved at 0.006 microseconds/frame (i.e., 3335 frames = 20.01 microseconds). The trajectories for the protein-membrane systems are compacted and centered. 
To load the trajectories on VMD, type the following in Tk console (7% PA trajectory for example):
$ mol new eVP40_7%PA_coords.gro 
 $ mol addfile eVP40_7%PA_traj.xtc waitfor all
