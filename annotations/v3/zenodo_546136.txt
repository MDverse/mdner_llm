MD simulation trajectory of a fully hydrated DPPG bilayer @314K: SLIPIDS, Gromacs 5.0.4. 2017.
MD simulation trajectory files, for fully hydrated DPPG bilayer [288 DPPG, 11232 WAT and 288 NA ions]. The SLIPIDS force field was used with Gromacs 5.0.4. Conditions: T=314K. 200 ns trajectory, last 100 ns uploaded for analysis.
