Dataset for BtuB TonB-dependent transporters and BtuG surface lipoproteins form stable complexes for vitamin B12 uptake in gut Bacteroides.

The dataset published here supports the work in the publication titled "BtuB TonB-dependent transporters and BtuG surface
lipoproteins form stable complexes for vitamin B12 uptake in gut Bacteroides." All the initial and final conformation files of the MD simulations are given along with the analyzed data presented in the manuscript.
