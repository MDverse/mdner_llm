LIPAC Test Dataset - MD Simulation Trajectories
Test dataset for LIPAC (Lipid-Protein Analysis with Causal Inference).
This dataset can be used for  test for other codes.
  Contains coarse-grained MD simulation trajectories of transmembrane peptide (EGFR TM sequence) in complex membrane
  with DIPC, DOPS, CHOL, SM, and GM3 lipids.
 
  Files included:
  - test_system.psf: System topology (62MB)
  - test_trajectory.xtc: MD trajectory (345MB)
  - README.md: Documentation
