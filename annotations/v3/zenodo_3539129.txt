Simulations of a DPPC bilayer at different temperatures

Simulations aimed to estimate the melting point of pure DPPC membranes in the Slipids force field. Simulations performed at 300, 305, and 310 K. The simulations were 500 ns (300 K & 305 K) or 300 ns (310 K) long. Full details of simulations available in the related manuscript, where they are also carefully analyzed:
Nanoscale Membrane Domain Formation Driven by Cholesterol
by Matti Javanainen, Hector Martinez-Seara, and Ilpo Vattulainen
Scientific Reports 7: 1143 (2017)
