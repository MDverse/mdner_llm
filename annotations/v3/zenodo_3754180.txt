HpTonB(30-285) simulation Amber ff03ws

MD simulation of periplasmic part of TonB protein from Helicobacter Pylori HpTonB(30-285) with Amber ff03ws force field without additional ions. Trajectory contains the last 1000ns of the total 1171ns simulation.
run1microsend.xtc (10 ps saving frequency), onlyPROTEIN.tpr, and confPROT.gro contain only protein.
run6-8skip.xtc (10 ps saving frequency), run6.tpr and confEND.gro contain also solvent.
Force field parameters are downloaded from https://github.com/bestlab/force_fields
