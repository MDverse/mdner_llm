Pn and Sn Tomographic Velocity Models of the North China Craton
This repository provides the Pn and Sn tomographic velocity models of the North China Craton derived from a large dataset of automatically picked and manually verified seismic phases.
The dataset includes:(1) gridded Pn and Sn velocity model files (TXT format), and(2) color palette table (CPT) files used for visualization in the main figures of the manuscript.
The released datasets allow independent reproduction of the principal tomographic images and facilitate further comparative or synthetic studies of the lithospheric structure beneath the North China Craton.
