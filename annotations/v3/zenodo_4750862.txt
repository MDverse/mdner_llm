MD simulation trajectory and related files for POPC bilayer with 350mM NaCl (CHARMM36, Gromacs 4.5)

Equilibrated POPC lipid bilayer simulation ran with Gromacs 4.5, CHARMM36, 60ns, T=303K, 72 POPC molecules, 2085 water molecules, 13 Na molecules, 13 Cl molecules. This data is used in the NMRLipids II project (nmrlipids.blospot.fi).
