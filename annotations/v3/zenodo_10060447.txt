Dataset for Simulation-guided engineering of split GFPs with efficient β-strand photodissociation

Files associated with performed experiments and simulations, including source data from experimental measurements and simulations, starting structures, parameters, simulation input files, and short videos of simulation trajectories. Simulation files include:Initial coordinates of the GFP chromophore for geometry optimization and RESP calculations in GAUSSIANParameter and starting files for QM/MM calculations around the conical intersection in TeraChemParameter, coordinate and input files for regular MD and simulated annealing simulations in AMBERParameter, coordinate and input files for umbrella sampling simulations of isomerization in AMBERResults files from TeraChemAdditionally, short videos are included of simulations of the cis and trans conformations.Experimental source data include: UV-vis data from strand-exchange experimentsMass spectrometry dataFluorescence data
