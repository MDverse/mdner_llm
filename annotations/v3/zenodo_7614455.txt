Simulation data for: "Unique Amphipathic a-helix Drives Membrane Insertion and Enzymatic Activity of ATG3"

Simulation data from Nishimura et al. (2023), "Unique Amphipathic a-helix Drives Membrane Insertion and Enzymatic Activity of ATG3".
The dataset contains the MD simulations executed for the Atg3/LC3/lipid membrane system, both in the WT and 5W-mutated variants.
More information can be found in the README file and in Table 1 of the cited paper.
