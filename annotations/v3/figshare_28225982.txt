Data of Network Pharmacology and Molecular Docking on TNF Signaling and Fibrotic Foci Reconfiguration by Phytoconstituents of Swertia chirayita
The spreadsheets and the Word files are the data generated to understand the biological activity of the whole plant, Swertia chirayita. The Word file contains the LC-MS/MS chromatograms, network pharmacology, molecular docking, including MD simulation, and Western blot band details. The Excel spreadsheet includes the raw data supporting the network pharmacology and the molecular docking. A Cytoscape software file included the whole screening of Swertia chirayita through network pharmacology. ZIP files contain the raw software data of Schrodinger molecular docking, dynamics, and principal component analysis; in vitro cell-based assay data; and complete original uncropped blots of Western blot analysis.
