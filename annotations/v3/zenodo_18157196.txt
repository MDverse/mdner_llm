Coarse-grained MD simulation data of Tau321-391 and Tau326-391 using SIRAH 2.0 force field
The dataset contains the MD simulation data associated with the article:
"Gradual N-terminal truncation of tau dGAE (297-391) reveals the importance of 321-325 sequence for early steps of fibril formation"
(not published yet)
 
Trajectories were produced using SIRAH 2.0 force field with reparametrization of water-protein interactions according to Martins et al. (https://doi.org/10.1021/acs.jcim.4c00965).  Initial conformers were generated by AlphaFold 2.
