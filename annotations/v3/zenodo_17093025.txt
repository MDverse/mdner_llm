Research data supporting: ''Observation of shuttling on the one-second timescale in a [10]cycloparaphenylene/C60 [2]catenane'

This repository contains the set of data and the code to reproduce the Molecular Dynamics (MD) results shown in: ''Observation of shuttling on the one-second timescale in a [10]cycloparaphenylene/C60 [2]catenane''
