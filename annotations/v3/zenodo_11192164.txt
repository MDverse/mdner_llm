Dataset- Unraveling the Molecular Complexity of N-Terminus Huntingtin Oligomers: Insights into Polymorphic Structures

This dataset contains self-assembly simulations of oligomeric huntingtin with varying polyQ lengths, related to "Unraveling the Molecular Complexity of N-Terminus Huntingtin Oligomers: Insights into Polymorphic Structures". Peptide systems are of N17+ polyQ, with polyQ adopting lengths between 7 to 45 repeats.
