MD setup data for ansamer amanitin derivatives and precursors for atroposelective synthesis

Setup files to reproduce the MD simulations for the amanitin derivatives 4a and 4b as well as the precursors 3b and 3c. The MD simulations were part of the study:
G. Yao, S. Kosol, M. T. Wenz, E. Irran, B. G. Keller, O. Trapp, R. D. Süssmuth, ChemRxiv 2022, DOI 10.26434/chemrxiv-2022-ll8lq.
For further instructions on the files, please refer to '0_README'.
