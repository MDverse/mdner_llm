POPC:POPG 1:1 MD simulation with CHARMM36 in 1 M CaCL solution and Na+ counter ions

CHARMM36 POPC:POPG 1:1 bilayer simulation in 1 M CaCl2 solution. The initial structure was taken from CHARMM-GUI. Temperature is 298 K. The system contains 150 POPC, 150 POPG, 150 sodium ions, 29766  TIP3 water molecules, 578 Ca2+ ions and  1156 Cl- ions.
Total length of simulation is 400 ns. First 100 ns were discarded. Saving frequency of 100-400ns.xtc is 20 ps.
