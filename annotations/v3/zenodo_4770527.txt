Simulating PIP2-induced gating transitions in Kir6.2 channels

Supplemental material to the study "Simulating PIP2-induced gating transitions in Kir6.2 channels", to be published in a Frontiers journal.
The upload contains three representative Molecular Dynamics simulation data sets, as described in the paper (6C3O, 6C3P, 6C3P L164P), comprising a compressed Gromacs .xtc, a .tpr, and the final step as .gro file, respectively.
