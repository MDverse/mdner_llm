KKEEVKKEEV(PK)7KEEVKKEEVKK (charged) disordered linker simulations with CHARMM36M
Classical MD simulations of the disordered linker with sequence KKEEVKKEEV(PK)7KEEVKKEEVKK (charged) disordered linker using CHARMM36M force field, run in water. Five replicas (mentioned as model01,02,03,04,05) were run with five different initial structures generated with IDPconfGen (Teixeira et al. (2022)).
