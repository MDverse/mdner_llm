Input parameters and output trajectory files for manuscript "Inhibitory Activity of Flavonoid Scaffolds on SARS-CoV-2 3CLPro: Insights from the Computational and Experimental Investigations"
Input parameters used for molecular dynamics simulations on GROMACS 2022 software and the output trajectory files for calculating binding free energy, in the manuscript with the title: "Inhibitory Activity of Flavonoid Scaffolds on SARS-CoV-2 3CLPro: Insights from the Computational and Experimental Investigations"
