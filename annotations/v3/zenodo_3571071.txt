Simulation of a POPE bilayer, lipid model based on OPLS-aa by Rog et al.

A 500 ns-long simulation of a bilayer consisting of 144 POPE lipids and 40 water molecules per lipid. All GROMACS-compatible input and output files are required. Topologies are provided by their original authors.
If you use the topologies, please cite the papers indicated in the POPE.itp file.
