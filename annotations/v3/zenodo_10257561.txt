Bathymetry, Magnetization and Acoustic Imagery Data of the Papua-Woodlark-Solomon Islands Region
4 Grids (Plus plots and colorscales):1. Bathymetry (relief)2. Acoustic imagery3. Magnetic anomaly4. MagnetizationThese are full grids of compiled marine geophysical data in the Papua-Woodlark-Solomon Islands region. Compilation of data up to 1999 is explained in Goodliffe et al. (1999). Since then data from Maurice Ewing 0002, Sonne 203 and Kilo Moana 0418 have been added.Associated publications:Benyshek, E. K., & Taylor, B. (2021).
