MM-MD and QM/MM-MD trajectories of para-nitrophenolate derivatives in aqueous solution

Classical MD (GAFF/TIP3P) performed of four para-nitrophenolate derivatives. All simulated in water for 600000 steps of 2 fs.
QM/MM-CPMD (BLYP/TIP3P) simulation of para-nitrophenolate for 44000 steps of 10 a.u.
QM/MM-BOMD (BLYP/TIP3P) simulation of three para-nitrophenolate derivatives for 44000 steps of 10 a.u.
Input files, output files and trajectories are provided for all of the above.
