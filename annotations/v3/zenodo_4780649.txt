MD simulation trajectories for paper "Binding of regulatory proteins to nucleosomes is modulated by dynamic histone tails"
The dataset includes all the MD simulation trajectories for the paper "Binding of regulatory proteins to nucleosomes is modulated by dynamic histone tails".
amber_OPC.zip contains the trajectories from simulation runs using AMBER forcefield and OPC water model.
charmm_TIP3P.zip  contains the trajectories from simulation runs using CHARMM forcefield and TIP3P water model.
