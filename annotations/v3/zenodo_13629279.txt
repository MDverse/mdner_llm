Mycobacterium tuberculosis FtsB and PerM interact via a C-terminal helix: MD trajectories and microscopy data
Data accompanying the manuscript "Mycobacterium tuberculosis FtsB and PerM interact via a C-terminal helix in FtsB to modulate cell division" currently published at bioRxiv at https://doi.org/10.1101/2024.03.11.584518
Molecular dynamics trajectories (only protein atoms to limit size) are provided to reproduce analysis in the paper.Raw microscope image data is provided to reproduce analysis and figures in the paper.
Other files and scripts required to reproduce the paper are available at https://github.com/smmlab/mtb-perm-ftsb
