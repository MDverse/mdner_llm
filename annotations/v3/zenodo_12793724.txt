Supplementary data for: "The Swiss Army Knife of Alginate Metabolism – Mechanistic Analysis of a Mixed-Function Polysaccharide Lyase/Epimerase of the Human Gut Microbiota"
This dataset comprises computational data associated with the research article titled "The Swiss Army Knife of Alginate Metabolism – Mechanistic Analysis of a Mixed-Function Polysaccharide Lyase/Epimerase of the Human Gut Microbiota". Included are trajectory files generated from molecular dynamics simulations, along with all extracted data utilized in the study.
