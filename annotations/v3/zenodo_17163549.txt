MD Simulation Data for the protein and RNA-Polymerase-peptide complexes
This dataset contains:
 
1. Modelled structures of RNA polymerase catalytic domain.
2. The input and output files from MD simulations performed on protein and protein-peptide complexes.
 
Each folder includes:
md_0_100.xtc - Trajectory file coordinates
structure of protein and docked protein-peptide complexes - initial model protein used for simulation
topol.top - Topology parameter
