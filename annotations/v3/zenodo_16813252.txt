Simulation of model mitochondrial membrane where TLCL is divided into TLPA+PG (CHARMM36)

MD simulation trajectories of model internal mitochondrial membrane (IMM) consisted of 45% PC (18:0, 20:4),SAPC, 35% PE (16:0, 18:1), POPE, and 20% CL (18:2), TLCL after complete separation of TLCL onto TLPA(-1) and TLPG(-1). The simulations are conducted in NPT ensemble at 310K in CHARMM36 force field using GROMACS v.2023+. High-temperature trajectory of 100 ns was taken as initial states (from timesteps 0, 50, 100 ns) for three deposited 1-us replicas.
Writing frequency is 20 ps.
Part of the bachelor project "Kardiolipin sin effekt på membranfluiditet" (in Norwegian) of Olivia Lykke Nygaard uploaded and referenced below.
