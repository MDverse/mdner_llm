MD simulation trajectory of POPC/CHOL (18.75 mol%) bilayer and related files

Simulation trajectory pre-equiblirated POPC/CHOL bilayer and related files from all-atom molecular dynamics simulations. The system contains 18.75mol% CHOL. Simulations have been performed with GROMACS-2021.2 with Lipid17 forcefield and TIP3P water model. The trajectory is 0-1000 ns, and we have disregarded the first 100 ns in our analysis.
The system in the trajectory consists of 104 POPC, 24 CHOL and 6400 water molecules. Temperature 310 K, pressure 1 bar.
Publication: https://doi.org/10.1016/j.bbamem.2022.183961
