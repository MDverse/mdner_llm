MD simulation of POPC bilayer with CHARMM36 force field. 20 w/l.

MD simulation of POPC (1-palmitoyl-2-oleoyl-phosphatidylcholine) bilayer with CHARMM36 force field. 20 w/l.
Dataset contains simulation files including trajectories (.xtc) (original production.xtc, and equilibrated and centered centered-pbcmol-100-500ns.xtc).
System: POPC bilayer in water
Number of lipids: 200 (100/leaflet)
Number of waters: 4000
Simulation time: 500 ns
Sampling rate: every 10 ps
Simulation engine: GROMACS 2019.5
Temperature: 300 K
