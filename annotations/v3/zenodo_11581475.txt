CG model for axolemma membrane
Model for axolemma membrane (preprint in "Molecular dynamics study of stiffness and rupture of axonal membranes" Majdolhosseini et al. 2024 https://doi.org/10.1101/2024.04.08.588595). See supplementary material for details on the bilayer composition. GROMACS (www.gromacs.org) input files:
equilibrated axolemma bilayer in GROMACS coordinate format (axolemma.gro)
topology files (topol.top and top.zip directory) for MARTINI 2.2 force field (from MARTINI webpage http://www.cgmartini.nl/)
example of GROMACS parameter file (relax.mpd)
Axolemma model has been designed by Maryam Majdolhosseini and Alessandra Villa
