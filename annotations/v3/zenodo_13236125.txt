CDNF simulation with amber99SBws (5 replicas)
MD simulations of CDNF with amber99SBws force field. Five replicas from different starting structures from taken from RCSB PDB: 4BIT (model 1, 2, 4, 6 and 8). Simulations were run wtih 100 mM NaCl at 298 K. The trajectory has a saving frequency of 10 ps.
