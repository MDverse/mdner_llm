Data for "Structure and mechanism of oxalate transporter OxlT in an oxalate-degrading bacterium in the gut microbiota"

MD simulation data of OxlT. Trajectory data and NAMD input files are included. The first 500 ns trajectory that starts from the occluded conformation with a transition to the outward-open conformation is in the OxlT-occ directory. The 200 ns trajectories that start from the outward-open conformation with different protonation states of K355 are in the  OxlT-out directory.
