Cytoplasmic Fluidity and the Cold Life: ProteomeStability is Decoupled from Viability in Psychrophiles
This dataset accompanies the article: 

"Cytoplasmic Fluidity and the Cold Life:  ProteomeStability is Decoupled from Viability in Psychrophiles".
By Beatrice Caviglia, Stepan Timr, Marianne Guiral, Marie-Thérèse Giudici-Orticoni, Tilo Seydel, Christian Beck, Judith Peters, Fabio Sterpone, Alessandro Paciaroni

The zip archive includes:

Starting configurations (start.gro) of the molecular dynamics (MD) simulations of P. arcticus and A. aeolicus
Final configurations (output.gro) of the molecular dynamics (MD) simulations of P. arcticus and A. aeolicus
Topology files of the Systems created with the charmm36-jul2022 forcefield.

Starting configurations of E. coli can be found at the Zenodo link: https://doi.org/10.5281/zenodo.7457333.
