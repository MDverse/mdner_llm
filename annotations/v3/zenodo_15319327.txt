Dataset for the article "Making Puzzle Pieces Fit or Reshaping MiMiC for Multiscale Simulations with CP2K and More"

This dataset contains additional material related to the article "Making Puzzle Pieces Fit or Reshaping MiMiC for Multiscale Simulations with CP2K and More": the source code of the MiMiC framework at the time of data gathering, simulation inputs and outputs, and a Jupyter notebook with the analysis scripts to reproduce the plots in the article.
