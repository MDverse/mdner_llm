Research data supporting: "Unsupervised Data-Driven Reconstruction of Molecular Motifs in Simple to Complex Dynamic Micelles"

This repository contains the set of data shown in the paper "Unsupervised Data-Driven Reconstruction of Molecular Motifs in Simple to Complex Dynamic Micelles", published on The Journal of Physical Chemistry B (DOI:10.1021/acs.jpcb.2c08726).
