Split the Charge Difference in Two! A Rule of Thumb for Adding Proper Amounts of Ions in MD Simulations

Despite
the relevance of properly setting ionic concentrations
in Molecular Dynamics (MD) simulations, methods or practical rules
to set ionic strength are scarce and rarely documented. Based on a
recently proposed thermodynamics method we provide an accurate rule
of thumb to define the electrolytic content in simulation boxes. Extending
the use of good practices in setting up MD systems is promptly needed
to ensure reproducibility and consistency in molecular simulations.
